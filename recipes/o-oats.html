Overnight Oats

Description

Simple and easy to make for breakfast and lunch

Ingrediants

Servings: 1

  • Oats 50g
  • Frozen berries 100g
  • Skyr Yoghurt 220g
  • Milk 200ml

Steps:

  1. Add the porridge into a container or jar that holds at least 700ml
  2. Add the frozen berries
  3. Add the Skyr Yoghurt
  4. Add the milk
  5. Leave it for 6 hours in the fridge before eating
