Tuna Mayo Pasta

Description

Simple and easy to make for lunch or dinner

Ingrediants

Servings: 1

  • Pasta 100g
  • Tin of Tuna 1
  • Mayonaise 1Tbsp

Steps:

  1. Boil some water and put into a pot where it will be brought back to boiling
  2. Add Pasta in and cook for 10-12mins
  3. Drain the water and allow pasta to cool down
  4. Add mayonaise and tuna (drained) and mix together in the pot
